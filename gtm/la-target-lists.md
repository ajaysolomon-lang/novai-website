## Sourcing Channels for Provider Lists

### Google Maps / Google Business Profile
- Search: "[service] in [LA neighborhood]"
- Capture: Business name, phone, website, rating, review count
- Priority: 4+ stars, 20+ reviews, actively responding
- Tool: Manual search or scraping tool
- Volume: 50-100 providers per category per neighborhood

### Yelp
- Search: "[service] near [zip code]"
- Capture: Business name, phone, website, Yelp rating
- Priority: Active businesses, recent reviews
- Note: Many already paying for Yelp ads = receptive to alternatives
- Volume: 30-80 per category per area

### Instagram
- Search: #LAplumber #LAcleaning #LApersonaltrainer etc
- Capture: Handle, follower count, service area, DM
- Priority: 500-10K followers (big enough to be real, small enough to want growth)
- Volume: 20-50 per category

### LinkedIn
- Search: "[Service] [Los Angeles]" in People
- Priority: B2B services (accounting, legal, IT, consulting)
- Volume: 30-50 per category

### Nextdoor
- Search: Business recommendations in LA neighborhoods
- Capture: Business name, service area, local reputation
- Priority: Frequently recommended by neighbors
- Volume: 10-30 per neighborhood

### Thumbtack/TaskRabbit/Angi
- Search: Active providers in LA
- Capture: Name, service, area (don't capture contact from platform)
- Then find them on Google/Yelp/Instagram for direct contact
- Priority: Providers with high ratings who are likely overpaying on fees

### Local Directories
- LA Chamber of Commerce member lists
- Better Business Bureau (BBB) LA listings
- Trade association directories (plumbing, electrical, HVAC)
- Local Facebook business groups

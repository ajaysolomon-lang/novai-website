## NBA (Next Best Action) Pricing Data Points

### Factors that influence job pricing:
1. **Service category** — base rate range
2. **Complexity/scope** — standard vs custom
3. **Urgency** — same-day premium (1.5-2x)
4. **Location within LA** — zip code variance
5. **Provider rating/experience** — premium for top-rated
6. **Recurring vs one-time** — discount for recurring
7. **Time of year** — seasonal demand spikes
8. **Comparable completed jobs** — historical data

### Seasonal Demand Patterns (LA)

| Month | Hot Categories | Demand Level |
|-------|---------------|--------------|
| Jan-Feb | Tax prep, organizing, deep cleaning | Medium |
| Mar-Apr | Landscaping, painting, spring cleaning | High |
